Our Story

CH Steel Overview And Partnership Story.

CH Steel is an incorporated company with equal shareholding between Culham Engineering (New Zealand) and The Herrick Corporation (USA), formed to deliver end-to-end steel services with integrated planning, fabrication, logistics, and erection under one operating model.

Partnership Story

Built On Shared Values And Delivery Discipline.

Built on shared values of integrity, transparency, and collaboration, the partnership combines local delivery depth with international-scale project experience to create practical value through certainty, quality, and programme control.

One Integrated Model

Planning, fabrication, logistics, and erection work as one delivery pathway instead of separate handoffs.

Equal-Shareholding Strength

Culham's local execution depth and Herrick's international steel experience combine under one incorporated operating framework.

Certainty In Delivery

The partnership is built to reduce delivery risk, protect programme, and improve outcomes on high-consequence projects.

Herrick Capabilities

International Scale And Technical Complexity Expertise.

High-Rise Expertise Airport + Civic Scale Power + Petrochemical Scale Seismic Delivery
  • Built on more than 100 years of engineering heritage, with over 30 years serving a global customer base across complex steel programmes.
  • Long-established steel fabrication and erection capability across high-rise, airport, and convention-centre work.
  • Significant delivery history in power and petrochemical projects, alongside commercial and high-rise structures.
  • Large global fabrication capacity and proven systems for complex programme delivery.
  • Deep execution experience in seismic environments and technically demanding structures.
  • Engineering and shop drawing workflows use TEKLA and AutoCAD with model-driven material control.
  • Certified quality and welding frameworks including ISO 9001:2015, AWS, AISC, CWB, LRQA, and SCA-aligned systems.

Culham Capabilities

New Zealand Delivery Strength With End-To-End Control.

Local Execution Depth 65+ Years Delivery History Heavy Haulage + Crane Technology-Led Fabrication
  • Established in 1958, with over 65 years of fabrication, construction, and project delivery experience.
  • Operations anchored by a 15-hectare Whangarei facility with additional North Island support capacity.
  • Direct harbour logistics access supports efficient import, export, and transport of fabricated equipment.
  • Integrated transport, heavy haulage, and crane capability for sequenced site delivery.
  • Technology-led fabrication workflows focused on precision, quality, and productivity.
  • Certified systems include ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, AS/NZS 5131, and ISO 3834-aligned controls.
