Social And Ethical Standards

Modern Slavery Statement

CH Steel is committed to ethical sourcing, supply chain transparency, and fair labour practices across all operations and project partners.

Our Position

  • Zero tolerance for forced labour, human trafficking, or exploitative working conditions.
  • Supplier expectations include compliance with all applicable legislation, particularly those relating to modern slavery and fair labour standards.
  • Procurement and project teams are expected to identify, report, and escalate ethical risks.

How We Apply This

  • Supply chain due diligence and risk awareness during sourcing and engagement.
  • Contractual and operational focus on ethical conduct and transparent documentation.
  • Continuous improvement through policy review and industry alignment.
