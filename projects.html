Project

Commercial Bay

Commercial Bay demonstrates CH Steel's integrated operating model in one of New Zealand's most constrained urban environments. The project involved approximately 8,500T of structural steel for a 39-storey commercial tower and retail complex in Auckland CBD, with an entirely welded braced perimeter frame and steel lift cores delivered through advanced erection sequencing ahead of concrete floor plates.
