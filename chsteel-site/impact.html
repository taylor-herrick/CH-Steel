Our Story

CH Steel Overview And Partnership Story.

CH Steel is a 50/50 joint venture between Culham Engineering (New Zealand) and The Herrick Corporation (USA), formed to deliver end-to-end steel services with integrated planning, fabrication, logistics, and erection under one operating model.

Partnership Story

Built On Shared Values And Delivery Discipline.

Built on shared values of integrity, transparency, and collaboration, the partnership combines local delivery depth with international-scale project experience to create practical value through certainty, quality, and programme control.

One Integrated Model

Planning, fabrication, logistics, and erection work as one delivery pathway instead of separate handoffs.

Joint-Venture Strength

Culham's local execution depth and Herrick's international steel experience combine under one operating framework.

Certainty In Delivery

The partnership is built to reduce delivery risk, protect programme, and improve outcomes on high-consequence projects.

Herrick Capabilities

International Scale And Technical Complexity Expertise.

High-Rise Expertise Airport + Civic Scale Seismic Delivery
  • Long-established steel fabrication and erection capability across high-rise, airport, and convention-centre work.
  • Large global fabrication capacity and proven systems for complex programme delivery.
  • Deep execution experience in seismic environments and technically demanding structures.

Culham Capabilities

New Zealand Delivery Strength With End-To-End Control.

Local Execution Depth Heavy Haulage + Crane Technology-Led Fabrication
  • One of New Zealand's largest fabrication facilities with significant annual steel throughput.
  • Integrated transport, heavy haulage, and crane capability for sequenced site delivery.
  • Technology-led fabrication workflows focused on precision, quality, and productivity.
