About CH Steel

One Integrated Team. End-To-End Structural Delivery.

CH Steel combines Culham Engineering and The Herrick Corporation capabilities into a single operating model for complex steel projects requiring precision, certainty, and accountability.

CH Steel Overview

CH Steel was established to unify strategy, engineering coordination, fabrication quality, and erection certainty under one delivery framework.

Culham + Herrick

The partnership merges regional knowledge with global project experience, giving clients deeper technical depth and stronger programme reliability.
